<div style="text-align: center;">

You're not a figment of my imagination,<br>
nor the stuff made of dreams.<br>
When the escapades of life come to a solemn standstill,<br>
or in restive times -<br>
times that seem to clutter in deep,<br>
shreds of the bygone moments often elusively peek<br>
into the undulating crevices,<br>
of the frescoes of time.<br><br>

And then, I yearn, often ache, to delve<br>
into the radiant realms<br>
on your ever-growing territory,<br>
seek solace from the scathing scruples of reality -<br>
slog through the fog,<br>
shrouding your misty entrance.<br><br>

But alas,<br>
all I get is a palate of colors - the dark and the bright.<br>
The sentient mind pervades deep into it,<br>
scouring for the sake of reminiscence. <br>
The celluloid offers in a sneak<br>
to a myriad host of moments,<br>
unravels beings who once walked the path -<br>
this very path -<br>
some who never cared,<br>
some who hated to the annals of hate,<br>
some who loved an ethereal love - <br>
same, yet different without them.<br><br>

I extend my arms to touch, feel and take in<br>
this very oblivious and apparent surrealism -<br>
but alas, curse the cruel dictates of time.<br>
Still, the window of the longing mind opens,<br>
threatens to transcend the barriers of space and time -<br>
fills in the senses with your rendering essence.<br>
And when, the dictates of time seep in, <br>
reducing my access to mere symbolism,<br>
all I get from you<br>
is a baffling assemblage of lights, sound, and blurry imagery -<br>
envisioning a well of depth - the depth of life.<br>
Memories --<br>
you're all I shall ever have<br>
to take on my way back,<br>
defying time through your colored glass.<br>

</div>
