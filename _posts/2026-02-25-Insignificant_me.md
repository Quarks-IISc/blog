title: "Insignificant me"

excerpt: "A romantic free verse on the feeling of insignificance in the vast cosmos, where only the love for another remains constant."

<div style="text-align: center;">

Amid the rustling chaos<br>
Through the hustle of the crowd<br>
A deadening silence rings my ears<br>
Unbearable, intolerable.<br><br>

I have come far, very far<br>
Things changed, dreams changed<br>
People changed, I changed<br>
The nature of my change changed<br>
Only my love for you remains unchanged<br><br>

I have not destroyed myself<br>
Maybe I have<br>
What can one say about oneself<br>
I judge myself in the light of my own morality<br>
But maybe I am not fair<br>
I forgive, time and time again, myself.<br>
I ask myself, I am worth it.<br>
Should I be forgiven?<br><br>

But<br>
In the cradle of this grand cosmos<br>
Does that really matter?<br>
Am I that significant<br>
To speak with truth<br>
No I am not<br>
None of us are<br><br>

Yet I have made myself think<br>
That I am the center of attention<br>
Oh how foolish I am.<br><br>

I look up at the sky<br>
At the inifinitude of stars<br>
Looking down at me<br>
Smiling staring maybe even laughing<br>
At my foolish narcissicm<br><br>

But they remind me again<br>
Of my insignificance<br>
In the infinity of this uknown universe<br>
I do not matter<br>
My insignificance, my foolishness, my hubris<br>
Nothing matters.<br>
Not to me anymore.<br><br>

Only you matter to me<br>
The light from your face dazzles my eyes<br>
The moon finally has a rival.<br><br>

I will not be here<br>
I will be gone<br>
But your eternal elegance will grace this place for eternity.<br>

</div>
