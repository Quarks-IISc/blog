title: "with bright eyes"
author: Sankalp Malkapure
categories: [poems]

excerpt: "A sunflower's dance with the distant sun. A story of growth, tropism, and the beautiful fallacy of a heart that burns while rooted to home."
---

<div style="text-align: center;">

with bright eyes i awoke,<br>
high in the vault i roamed;<br>
with fallen fallacy i chose,<br>
while, rooted still, she faced to home.<br><br>

through passing hours, she traced my ardent way,<br>
she drank in silence all my wandering ray;<br>
leading and brightening all along,<br>
while, bound to her, i burned so long.<br><br>

over the tropism, she dallied the arc,<br>
however, when i lingered back, she feared embark;<br>
reaching the far rim, i dimmed along,<br>
while, bound to her, i burned so long.<br><br>

the growth led only to shun-<br>
the yearning sunflower, the distant sun.

</div>
