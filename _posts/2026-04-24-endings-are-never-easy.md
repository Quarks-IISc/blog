<div style="text-align: center;">

Endings are never easy, are they?<br>
Realizing things are ending<br>
Days are about to change<br>
And faces to be replaced<br><br>

The days when we laughed together<br>
The nights we cried together<br>
The days we felt victorious<br>
The nights we felt lost<br><br>

It's time, though<br>
Time to walk ahead<br>
Time to begin again<br>
Time to become who I once dreamed of<br><br>

So, go ahead and<br>
Take that one last walk through the corridors<br>
Cycle through the old routes<br>
Share a moment with that same group<br>
And remember.<br><br>

How lucky I feel<br>
To be privileged to miss some people<br>
To have walked the same roads alongside<br>
To have shared a piece of my life<br><br>

It's not sad though<br>
Having grown through these years<br>
Collecting memories from bits and pieces<br>
Memories were always strange, weren't they?<br><br>

And endings are never easy, are they?

</div>
