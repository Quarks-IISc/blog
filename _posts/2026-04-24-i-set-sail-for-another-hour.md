<div style="text-align: center;">

Here! sets sail another hour,<br><br>

In Gulmohar's bloom speaks the spring,<br>
'A page afresh, now you shall turn,<br>
Of bygone hours may you sing.'<br><br>

A time apt to reflect and pause<br>
The fourth hour of a rosy dawn,<br>
To turn around to behold the past,<br>
of all I gained and all I lost.<br><br>

Now in this fair realm of mine,<br>
I have no friends but only two,<br>
For Faith and solitude, stood by me<br>
when there stood with me few.<br><br>

In a dazed state, o'er shadowed ways,<br>
With not a soul to shine a light,<br>
On solitude's counsel I relied,<br>
And stepped out fearless from the night<br><br>

If all the shackles I did melt,<br>
And through the clouds I could thunder;<br>
If faith has brought me this far,<br>
Then faith shall sail me yonder.<br><br>

But in raging storms and burning trials,<br>
Whose soothing hand I felt so near?<br>
'Twas God who chose the course to steer,<br>
And stilled the tide of every fear.<br><br>

Fortune's favourite I've not been<br>
But by grace, afore the curtains fell<br>
I emerged on the centre stage,<br>
A hero who has been through hell.<br><br>

And Here! I sang of bygone hours,<br>
A page afresh I'm ready to turn,<br>
May the Gulmohar bless my soul,<br>
I paused to reflect, and now 'tis done!

</div>
