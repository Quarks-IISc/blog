<div style="text-align: center;">

Were you the one, who stopped by me<br>
When I was serenading to the moon?<br><br>

Or when I was getting some dirt o'er my hands<br>
Chaining my bicycle in the afternoon?<br><br>

Were you the one, by the sunny shore<br>
Gracing a tiara of strung olive twigs<br><br>

Or a walk in the mist by a hilly terrain<br>
Tracing a sweet tune on your apple-red lips?<br><br>

Do you too believe; the world is good<br>
Or fancy a train journey for the whistle blow<br><br>

Or did you ever think of me at the sights,<br>
Of falling stars and of fireflies' glow?<br><br>

For why does it seem I have loved your smile<br>
Since when I first felt, the urge to write<br>

</div>
