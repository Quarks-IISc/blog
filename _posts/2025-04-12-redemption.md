Poet: Rohith R

<div style="text-align: center;">

A quiet Saturday night<br>
Marks the end of my plight.<br>
The mist has cleared, at last revealed<br>
The monster that hunted me.<br><br>

For months it played with my heart.<br>
Twisted and broke apart<br>
It drove me mad, needlessly sad<br>
All the while, in deep disguise.<br><br>

I blamed my near and dear ones<br>
For having loved me once.<br>
I doubted the fair maiden,<br>
Who, my mind, had shaken.<br><br>

But all in all, feeding me gall,<br>
It was in myself, like a nimble wood elf,<br>
A playwright's boon, my mental typhoon.<br>
But no more shall I hide.<br><br>

From the monster that waits inside.<br>
I have survived the monstrous tide.<br>
And with its own power shall it misguide.<br>
The worst has passed.<br><br>

It has been ousted at last.<br>
My heart smiles with glee<br>
I am finally free.<br><br>

"This feeling, inside me.<br>
Finally found my love, I finally broke free<br>
No longer torn in two,<br>
I'd take my own life before losing you..."<br>

</div>
