Poet: Debosmita Biswas

<div style="text-align: center;">

On a rainy night<br>
When everything was dark<br>
There was no sight<br>
Or a single mark<br><br>

Mark of the sin that occurred<br>
Just yesterday, not so long ago<br>
But because she was a girl and so...<br>
The man decided that she must go<br><br>

On a rainy night<br>
when everything was dark<br>
When nothing was bright<br>
And only the strays would bark<br><br>

She was returning from her night shift<br>
Then, a cruel shadow, a secret gift<br>
Gift, colder than the midnight rain<br>
When all her hard work went to vain<br><br>

The years she fought to spend,<br>
The exams she conquered,<br>
The struggles she overcame---<br>
All that labour went in vain.<br><br>

All her labour went in vain<br>
On that rainy night,<br>
When everything was dark<br>
She was 21<br>
On her way for a long run<br><br>

And soon enough the whispers began:<br>
"What was she wearing that night?"<br>
"She was definitely asking for it."<br><br>

On a rainy night<br>
When everything was dark<br>
She had lost her life<br>
With lots of marks<br><br>

Marks on her character, purity<br>
On her career and dignity<br>
Marks that etched into her<br>
So painfully<br><br>

And yet people stop her<br>
Stop her to voice out<br>
Stop her to protest<br>
Tell her that she's not pure<br>
Not trustworthy, immature<br><br>

Later that night<br>
When the man<br>
fulfilled his desires<br>
He left her, alone, undressed<br><br>

On that same rainy night<br>
When it was pitch dark<br>
She trembled in pain<br>
Realising that now<br>
The vow was broken<br><br>

The vow to be the ideal<br>
An ideal daughter who obeyed everyone<br>
An ideal sister for every brother she met<br>
An ideal wife who was pure before marriage<br>
An ideal mother who sacrificed herself<br><br>

Chasing the ideal<br>
She forgot herself<br><br>

Now in the midst of the rain<br>
She now doesn't feel any pain<br>
Because in death there's peace<br>
For now, the trembling ceased---<br><br>

For in the dark peace of death, there's ease<br>

</div>
