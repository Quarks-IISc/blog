Poet: Jahin

<div style="text-align: center;">

By the pedestal, I sit here today, all alone<br>
Waiting for my beloved to come along<br>
Years have passed, seasons have changed<br>
She has not come yet, I am waiting for her arrival<br>
Sitting eagerly by the same spot is the only thing, unchanged.<br><br>

I look with hope at the lavenders<br>
The way they shake their heads in joy in the gentle wind<br>
The sweet memories come back to my mind<br>
The days when we both shared the same joyous moments<br>
Under the vivid moonlight<br>
The lovely sunset and all the beauties of mother nature<br><br>

Now she lives her days happily, not with me<br>
But I still sit today in this dark night waiting for her to come back
one day<br>
To soothe this broken heart<br>
And bring me out of this melancholy.<br><br>

I remember her, still now, vividly.<br>
Her face, under the moonlight,<br>
Had made the moon jealous<br>
Her glowing eyes in the moonlight had summoned the gods from heaven.<br>
Her lips, strawberry, delicate as the petals of a rose.<br><br>

I know, she might not come back to me<br>
But I still sit here near the pedestal, waiting for her, all alone, in
the beaming moonlight,<br>
With a forlorn smile!<br>

</div>
