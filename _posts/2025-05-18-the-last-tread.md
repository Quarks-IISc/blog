Poet: Rekha Nawal Jyoti

<div style="text-align: center;">

"Here you are"<br>
"Take the key"<br>
"You will find it in the desk"<br>
In a far corner<br>
Rested the desk<br><br>

"Go ahead"<br>
"Take the key"<br>
"Waste not the time ahead"<br>
On the left wall<br>
ticked the clock<br><br>

"Belongs to you"<br>
"Take the key"<br>
"Soon enough he will arrive"<br>
Between the door and<br>
Desk, stood I<br><br>

"The lock!!"<br>
"Take the key"<br>
"You are just a step away"<br>
Desk opened and<br>
Footsteps were heard<br><br>

"Yours it was"<br>
"He took the key"<br>
"There is the door, leave"<br>
I stood there<br>
As I was ...<br>

</div>
