Look at the way he waits, standing so still at the edge of the platform while the rest of the station moves around him. Whatever he had to do out there in the noise and the dust, that chapter is finally closed. 
The day is done, the weight is lifted, and now there's nothing left to do but watch the tracks and wait for the train that will finally take him home.
