Poet: Nikunj Goel

<div style="text-align: center;">

I was one, when I was left in this cruel world.<br>
She went away in those far reaches of sky,<br>
Among those twinkling stars<br>
I lost her when I even could not speak<br>
The word 'Mother.'<br><br>

As the tide of the time of flowed,<br>
It also erased my old memories.<br>
I had only seen her in the image hung on the opposite wall.<br>
I sat under it gazing it for hours,<br>
Wishing had she come out of the image<br>
And blessed me with her love,<br>
But she never came to quench my thirst.<br><br>

Whenever I asked my sister<br>
How was our mother?<br>
She always replied that she was the same like you, My Brother.<br>
With these words I felt my mother's essence within me.<br>
As if telling me, "You have a long way to go on my child."<br><br>

Every night I used to lie on the roof<br>
With a pillow beneath me<br>
And she used to lull me<br>
With the cold breeze which flew over me.<br>
I knew she was among those<br>
A hundred thousand stars,<br>
Reflecting her light as a token of love on mе.<br>

</div>
