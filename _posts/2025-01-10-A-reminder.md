Poet: Aditi Mishra

<div style="text-align: center;">

Of life in the world<br><br>

Faint memories that remain,<br>
Like a cradle in the sand,<br>
Drops of water that sustain,<br>
Of courage in Earth,<br>
That lights up our hearts,<br>
Like a brilliant spark,<br>
Making way in the dark;<br>
Of Love,<br>
A little star among billions,<br>
On its own and unique,<br>
A peaceful legacy for eons,<br>
Distant yet complete.<br>

</div>
